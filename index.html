About IDR

Built for digital risk roles that demand confidence and control.

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber, technology, and AI risk roles in regulated and high-consequence environments. We help students, early-career professionals, and existing practitioners build the judgement, technical fluency, and governance mindset needed to operate where resilience matters most.

IDR connects academic insight with real-world delivery through UK university partnership and employer-facing projects. This model gives learners exposure to live methods, practical frameworks, and sector expectations while giving organisations access to talent shaped by current industry practice.
